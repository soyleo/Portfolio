About Me

My career is defined by one word: impact. Since my beginnings in musical theater and digital literacy, I noticed that the success of any system depends on the perfect synchrony between technique and audience.

I have led communities and large-scale events in San Juan, notably coordinating the province's first Medieval Fair "Castillos, Espadas & Brebajes", a milestone event that exceeded all attendance forecasts. This management capacity and my vocation for knowledge sharing led me to serve as an educator and educational strategy planner at Vinci Institute, training new generations in the foundations of game development.

I brought that same strategic vision to the global industry, working for over 7 years for giants like Disney (Kongregate) and Hutch. In studios such as Dedalord and Trick, I analyzed critical KPIs to triple revenue on international projects and led technical teams in high-pressure environments. Today, I apply all that background—from pedagogy to Senior Game Design—to build independent web solutions where transparency, efficiency, and scalability are the priority.
